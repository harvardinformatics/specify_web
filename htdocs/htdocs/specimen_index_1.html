Index of Botanical Specimens

Of the over 5 million specimens in the Harvard University Herbaria, over 210,000 have records in the Specify 6 database system of which about 100,000 correspond to type specimens of vascular plants and mosses. Also included are fungi, lichens, bryophytes, and algae.
