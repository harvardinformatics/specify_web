Databases

The Gray Herbarium Index of New World Plant Names The Gray Index (in part) is now incorporated into the International Plant Names Index
The International Plant Names Index IPNI is a joint project with the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew and the Australian National Herbaria to provide an online database of the names and associated basic bibliographical details of all vascular plants.
All of the lists below are generated from sources that include vascular plants and cryptogams.
Botanists Authors of botanical names, collectors of botanical specimens, and authors of botanical publications.
Publications Titles and abbreviations of botanical publications with authors, dates of publication and BPH and TL2 references.
Specimens Specimen records, primarily type specimens from the Harvard University Herbaria and New England Botanical Club served from our Specify6 specimen database.
